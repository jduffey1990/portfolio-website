Hello, and welcome to my site. My name is Jordan Duffey

I started my working life as a dentist. After 4 years in private practice, I decided that being in health care wasn't my true calling. I decided that I wanted my career to fit my skills better, and to curate my career around my life, not the other way around. I now am pursuing a career in software development in Denver, CO. Things that drew me to the job of being a software developer include:

  • Freedom to work remotely or in the office depending on the specific job.
  • Decent career pay with lots of flexibility.
  • Being able to solve a new problem, or differing parts of a larger problem, every single day.
  • I have always been talented at math and I have loved learning languages, so programming gives me the chance to demonstrate weilding algorithms and learning many digital languages.

About Jordan

Jordan was born in Tucson, AZ and moved to Colorado Springs early in life. He attended college at UCLA and earned a B.S. in Psychobiology as a predental major. He returned home to attend the University of Colorado School of Dental medicine, where he gratuated summa cum laude with honors. After dental school, he did a General Practice Residency staying at CU to pursue extended learning in implant dentistry, endodontics, and other more in depth general dental procedures.

After 4 years of practice in the Greater Denver Area, Jordan decided that he wasn't truly meant to pursue a life in health care. It was a difficult decision, but his stress levels and day to day enjoyment of life increased. After some introspection and a greater understanding of his strengths and interests, Jordan decided to become a programmer. He did some free education online and some paid courses through Udemy to solidify his interest in coding.
